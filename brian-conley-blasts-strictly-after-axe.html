    The live audience gasped as he leapt on the judges’ podium, saying: “They only spend £25 on the toilet.”

    And host Tess Daly looked stunned as he referred to judge Darcey Bussell, who had voted him off, as “the horny one”.

    He then went on to praise his brother Alan who is a floor manager on the show.

    BBC chiefs edited out his outburst before screening tonight’s results show.
