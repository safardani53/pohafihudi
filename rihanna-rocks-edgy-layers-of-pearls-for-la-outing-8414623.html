    Rihanna Rocks Edgy Layers of Pearls on L.A. Outing

    13 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Rihanna's style is consistently iconic, even when she's evoking decades past.

    While rocking a '90s look out in Los Angeles on Sunday night, the artist, 35, wore a multi-strand pearl choker paired with a sheer lingerie top and baggy blue jeans under a faux fur-lined black trench.

    The layered jewelry look, which covered her entire neck and décolletage, included strands that hung past her waist.

    She wore her hair piled high in a bun, which further emphasized her bold accessories, and completed her glamorous street chic with black creeper sneakers from her Fenty x Puma collaboration.

    Rihanna's partner and father of her two boys, A$AP Rocky, 35, was recently announced as Puma's creative director, and the pair turned up in Las Vegas last month over the Formula 1 Grand Prix weekend for the Puma x F1 collection launch.

    For the collection's launch event, the "Love on the Brain" singer wore a similar look, donning a brown ankle-length coat, silver earrings, necklaces and an anklet. She wore her hair in a similar high bun style.

    Rihanna has long been involved with Puma, having first launched the Fenty x Puma collaboration in 2014. She later took over as Puma's creative director, as reported by Time.

    As for her most recent sneaker collaboration, the unisex shoe – out since Sept. 15 – also includes kids’ sizes. “This time around, we expanded our creations to kids, ’cause I selfishly want my boys in everything their dad and I wear or design," Rihanna told Vogue in September.

    The Grammy winner gave birth to Riot Rose, 4 months, on Aug. 1, and elder son RZA Athelston is 19 months.

    Being back at Puma, Rihanna told Vogue, is "a bit of a family reunion."

    “Coming back to Puma as a mom, in a new space, allowed for new perspectives and ideas," she added.
