    From her starring role on ‘Dawson’s Creek’, to raising her now-teenage daughter Suri as a single parent, look back on Katie Holmes’ transformation over the years!

    Katie Holmes has been a household name since she burst onto the scene on ‘Dawson’s Creek’. After playing Joey Potter for all 6 seasons, the actress played Rachel Dawes opposite Christian Bale in the hit movie Batman Begins. Although she did have success with a number of smaller projects through the 2000s, Katie’s personal life took center stage when she married actor Tom Cruise in 2006. The pair had a highly publicized relationship and welcomed their daughter, Suri Cruise in April 2006. By 2012, however, the couple divorced and Katie has since dedicated her time to giving Suri a normal childhood. She’s also continued to appear on screen in films like ‘Logan Lucky’, ‘All We Had’, and appeared in 11 episodes of the series ‘Ray Donovan’.
