    GWEN Stefani has received very mixed opinions over yet another eye-catching look.

    The No Doubt alum shared glam pics on Instagram on Monday, but not everyone was won over by her outfit choice.

    In the post, Gwen, 54, showed off one of her bold ensembles for The Voice.

    The television star posed in her dressing room, pouting as she directed her sultry gaze away from the camera.

    Gwen had her blond hair tied back, with a messy bun high on her head while two curled strands fell in front of her face.

    She wore a full face of flawless makeup, with false lashes reaching almost up to her brows, glittery eyeshadow, and a pink lipstick.

    Adding even more sparkle, Gwen was covered in crystal jewelry, with her nails blinged out with gems.

    For her gown, the pop star wore a dress entirely covered in feathers, dyed light pastel shades of blue and pink.

    The feathers covered her whole body, floating around her strapless bodice and wide, full skirt.

    In the comment section, however, Gwen's social media followers had a love-hate relationship with the look.

    One person exclaimed: "This look for the win!! It’s beyond!!!"

    A second agreed: "A very beautiful dress! Gwen is amazing as always," along with fire and heart emojis.

    However, another fan wrote: "My fist thought was ostrich."

    "Mine was Christmas turkey," joked another social media follower.

    A fifth person also remarked: "For a second I thought it was one of the Kardashians. What a pity!"

    KEEPING UP WITH GWEN

    This isn't the first time Gwen has been compared to the Kardashians in recent days.

    Last week, the star posted more behind-the-scenes shots, and fans were quick to note the changes in her appearance.

    In the photos, Gwen posed against a wooden door, smiling widely in a plunging green sequin dress.

    However, fans soon slammed her appearance, with one snarking: "Another plastic Kardashian clone. A sad loss of a person."

    A second agreed: "I didn't even know that was her at first!"

    "You can’t stop! You look so fake yourself," blasted a third.

    A final commenter added: "Too much fakery for me!

    'TWEAKMENTS'

    While fans think Gwen has dramatically changed her appearance, the star herself hasn't confirmed whether she's gone under the knife.

    In 2004, Gwen told People: “I’m hoping my children will save me from my vanity. If it doesn’t, plastic surgery is an option… It sucks to have to grow older. We all have to accept it.”

    However, one doctor shared his beliefs that Gwen was already deep in plastic surgery, and wasn't "accepting" the ageing process.

    Dr. Richard Westreich - a top New York-based facial plastic surgeon - told The U.S. Sun: “Gwen looks to have spent around $100,000 on cosmetic procedures and tweakments, which means she won't need a facelift in ten years' time.

    “But she’s at that inflection point where the motivation should be to look fabulous and age-appropriate, and while she looks good right now, I hope she doesn't over-pursue and end up looking bizarre rather than great for her age.
