    PAWN Stars' Chumlee was hospitalized with severe abdominal pain and breathing troubles in Las Vegas on Monday.

    The reality star, whose real name is Austin Russell, joked that he had coronavirus as he shared a snap of himself lying in his hospital bed on Instagram.

    Speaking to fans on his Instagram Stories, the 37-year-old said: "What's up guys... stuck here in the hospital.

    "Probably got coronavirus."

    He added in another clip that doctors were still running tests, joking that he might just need to go to the toilet.

    "So far all tests are good. they're going to run some more but it looks like I think it looks like I just need a poop," Chumlee quipped.

    Chumlee also said it'd been a "tuff day" while posting a photo of himself in his hospital gown.

    The TV star hit the headlines last year when he lost a staggering 100 pounds thanks to a gastric sleeve surgery.

    Chumlee, who married Olivia Rademann in August, has been candid about his weight battle in the past.
