    Shoppers are rushing to pick up Odeon, Vue and Cineworld tickets for 3 for National Cinema week -

    DO you enjoy going to the cinema but are put off by the price? Odeon, Vue and Cineworld are selling 3 tickets on Saturday for adults and kids in honour of National Cinema Day so it could be your chance to bag a bargain.

    28 May 2024, 00:00

    DO you enjoy going to the cinema but are put off by the price?

    Odeon, Vue and Cineworld are selling £3 tickets on Saturday for adults and kids in honour of National Cinema Day so it could be your chance to bag a bargain.

    Over 600 cinemas across the country including major chains and smaller independent venues will be offering the cut-price deal.

    If you haven’t seen the summer’s two smash hits, Barbie or Oppenheimer, now could be the moment.

    The Odeon website states: “Watch great movies from just £3 on 2 September.

    “All films, all screens, all day — all from £3 across all our cinemas.

    More on cinemas

    “That means all our biggest films including Barbie, Oppenheimer, The Equalizer 3, Blue Beetle, Strays, Haunted Mansion and more. 

    “There's something for everyone from just £3.”

    The discounted tickets will cost £3 if you buy them at the cinema, but it’s important to know that some cinemas are charging £4 online (to include a £1 booking fee).

    Many parents have raved about the deal, with one saying: “I booked to see the barbie film for my girls.”
