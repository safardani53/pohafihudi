    REALITY TV star, Kim Zolciak, is known as one of the original cast members of Bravo's The Real Housewives of Atlanta. 

    After it was revealed in May 2023 that the 45-year-old singer had filed for divorce from her husband, Kroy Biermann, fans were shocked to hear that in July the pair had decided to, instead, remain together.

    How many kids does Kim Zolciak have?

    Kim Zolciak has welcomed a total of six children over the course of two marriages.

    The TV personality has three daughters - Brielle Karenna, Ariana Lenee, and Kaia Rose - as well as three sons - Kroy "KJ" Jagger Jr., Kash Kade, and Kane Ren.

    Her eldest daughter, Brielle Karenna, was born on February 25, 1997. Her biological father's identity has yet to be revealed.

    The 25-year-old is a model who started her own makeup line back in 2019 called, KAB Cosmetics.

    Her second daughter, Ariana Lenee, was born on October 17, 2001, to Zolciak and her ex-husband, Daniel Toce.

    She has regularly been featured in the Real Housewives of Atlanta and is known for having documented her weight loss journey of over 50 pounds after reportedly struggling with her body image and facing bullying at school.

    On May 31, 2011, Zolciak welcomed her first son, Kroy "KJ" Jagger Jr., with her second husband, Kroy Biermann.

    She and Biermann also share three other children together. Zolciak's second son, Kash, was born on August 15, 2012, while her twins, Kaia Rose and Kane Ren, were welcomed on November 24, 2013.

    Who has Kim Zolciak been married to?

    In 2001, Zolciak tied the knot with Daniel Toce.

    After welcoming their daughter, Ariana, the pair divorced in 2003.

    In 2011, Zolciak married former NFL player, Kroy Biermann, in Georgia.

    Two years later, Biermann successfully adopted Zolciak's daughters Brielle and Ariana.

    Is Zolciak getting divorced?

    On May 8, 2023, it was revealed that Zolciak filed for divorce from her husband of 11 years.

    According to legal documents obtained by TMZ, the pair's date of separation is reportedly listed as April 30.

    Zolciak said her and Biermann's marriage is "irretrievably broken with no hope of reconciliation."

    She reportedly asked for primary physical custody of the kids, joint legal custody, and spousal support.

    However, on July 7, 2023, a source told TMZ that the pair had decided to call off their divorce.

    According to the outlet: "Our sources say Kim and Kroy believe they can fix their relationship with just face-to-face conversations, and a little help from God ... as they're relying on their shared Christian faith to push through the issues that brought them to the brink of divorce."

    A source told Us Weekly: "They’re getting along so [Kim] called it off," noting that their relationship status may change again in the future. “Tomorrow might be different.”

    During a July 10 episode of SiriusXM’s Andy Cohen Live, Andy Cohen admitted he was "surprised" to hear the news, saying: “I was really surprised to hear Kim and Kroy got back together, especially after, like, a half-hour talk with her a few weeks ago, and it sounded like irreconcilable differences at that time."
