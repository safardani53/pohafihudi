    Who Are These New Twins?

    News broke in Nov. 2012 that Max and Charlie — who starred as the grown-up versions of Lynette’s twins on Desperate Housewives — would be joining Teen Wolf‘s third season as twin werewolves Ethan and Aiden.

    Along with being crazy ripped, as seen in the above photo, Ethan and Aiden are described as being “dangerous charmers with brilliant minds.” You know, because Beacon Hills totally needs more of those.

    Max and Charlie join a few other new faces in season three, including Neighbours‘ Adelaide Kane as the “mysterious” Cora and Felisha Terrell as “ruthless, sexy” Alpha wolf Kali.

    HollywoodLifers, how do you feel about these two new additions? Will Max and Charlie’s combined shirtless-ness make up for the lack of half-naked Jackson in season three, or will you forever mourn the loss of Colton? Drop me a comment with your thoughts!
