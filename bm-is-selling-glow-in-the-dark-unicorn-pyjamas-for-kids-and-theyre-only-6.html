    BEDTIMES just got a whole lot more magical as B&M has launched a range of glow in the dark unicorn pyjamas – and they’re amazing!

    There will be no trouble getting little ones to bed as they will be desperate to get into these PJs.

    The pink unicorn pyjamas are only £6 and illuminate at night, making them the perfect solution for those who are scared of the dark.

    They’re made from a comfy fleece material which will keep the kids warm, especially whilst the weather is still disappointingly cold and dull in some areas of the UK.

    And for the rare few who aren’t into unicorns, the twin set is also available in a star print edition.

    Everyone’s favourite budget store launched a glow in the dark bedroom range earlier this year.

    The pink unicorn collection features a rug, cushion, a throw and duvet set – and prices start from just £5.99.

    When the discount retailer first revealed the sell-out bedroom range on their Instagram page, it caused chaos with their customers.

    Users were commenting how much they “needed” the range for their children and the post racked up more than 44,000 views in total.

    Head to your nearest B&M store to get your hooves from items in the range.
