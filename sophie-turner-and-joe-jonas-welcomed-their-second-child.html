    The British actress Sophie Turner just gave birth to her second daughter with musician Joe Jonas, making her the mother of two little girls. Representatives for Turner, 26 years old, and Jonas, 32 years old, verified the news, stating that both parents are delighted with the birth of their daughter. The Game of Thrones actor and Joe Jonas are already parents’ daughter Willa, who was born on July 22, 2020, and is already one year old. Although the couple has been seen with their daughter on many occasions, they have opted to conceal her identity for privacy concerns.

    Sophie Turner revealed her pregnancy In May

    Turner and Jonas were spotted many times in public prior to her announcement of her pregnancy. In a May interview, the actress announced that she and her husband were expecting their second child. In a May interview with Elle UK, the actress of The Staircase discussed her pregnancy and the enthusiasm around it.

    “For me, life is all about nurturing the next generation. The greatest joy in life is seeing my kid grow stronger over time. We are thrilled to be growing our family. This is the greatest gift ever.”

    She also described Willa’s reaction to her mother’s expanding tummy, noting that the toddler was “taking her territory.” The actress continued:

    “I’ll indicate my tummy and ask, “What’s inside?” And she will say, “Baby.” Then she will point to her own stomach and say, “Baby,” before pointing to her father’s stomach and saying, “Baby.” So, I believe she just believes that a belly is a baby and that this is its name. But she seems far more possessive than usual, so I believe she has some idea. She wants her mother constantly.”

    Since the announcement, Sophie Turner has made a number of dazzling public appearances while carrying her pregnant tummy. At the Met Gala in 2022, she donned a black Louis Vuitton with diamond accents over her chest and Louis Vuitton’s High Jewelry to complete her ensemble.

    The actress also discussed parenthood on the podcast Unwrapped. She said that the recent changes in her life have given her a “fresh perspective” as an actor. “The notion of family has significantly increased my emotionality. The level of empathy is elevated, and the sentiments for family are almost overwhelming. Becoming a mother has enhanced my acting skills.”
