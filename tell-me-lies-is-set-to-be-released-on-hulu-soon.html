    Tell Me Lies, Hulu’s upcoming drama series, will premiere on the site on September 7, 2022. The program is about a couple who are in a toxic relationship and how it affects them emotionally as well as the people around them. Tell Me Lies is set to premiere on Hulu on September 7, 2022, at 12:01 a.m. ET (tentative). The first three episodes are planned to be published on the same day, with the remaining episodes following on Wednesdays.

    Grace Van Patten and Jackson White play the key roles in the show. Continue reading to discover more about Hulu’s Tell Me Lies teaser and other features.

    Tell Me Lies on Hulu is the tale of a turbulent relationship.

    A Hulu trailer of the series depicts the protagonist heroine Lucy wandering around her campus for the first time and encountering her future boyfriend Stephen for the first time. Sparks fly instantly when the two establish eye contact. The trailer has a romantic undertone but also a disturbing one, since there’s an air of mystery around Stephen and Lucy, implying that their possible relationship may not be all that pleasant. Viewers may look forward to an unflinchingly honest and personal picture of a troubled relationship. It’s anticipated to be a character-driven romance drama about the harsh realities and ugly truths of love. According to the show’s official synopsis:

    “Tell Me Lies” depicts a turbulent yet enthralling romance over the course of eight years. When Lucy Albright and Stephen DeMarco meet in college, they are at that formative age when apparently little decisions have irreversible implications.”

    The description continues,

    “Although their relationship begins like any other campus romance, they quickly become entangled in an addictive entanglement that will forever alter not only their lives, but the lives of everyone around them.”

    More information about the Tell Me Lies cast

    Grace Van Patten plays Lucy in the show. Van Patten seems to be extremely remarkable in the series trailer. She easily conveys the fundamental agony and despair that consumes her character. Viewers should expect the celebrity to provide an outstanding performance. Grace Van Patten is best recognized for her roles in The Meyerowitz Stories, Nine Perfect Strangers, and other films directed by Noah Baumbach.

    The series also stars noted actor Jackson White as Lucy’s love interest, Stephen DeMarco. White looks stunning in the clip, portraying an air of mystery and intrigue that characterizes his equation with Lucy. White has starred in movies and television series such as Ambulance, Mrs. Fletcher, and SPF-18. Aside from the aforementioned performers, the film also includes a number of others in key supporting parts, including:

    • Catherine Missal
    • Spencer House
    • Benjamin Wadsworth
    • Sonia Mena
    • Natalee Linez

    Carola Lovering’s novel of the same name inspired the series. Meaghan Oppenheimer, well known for her work on We Are Your Friends and Fear the Walking Dead, is the creator and showrunner for Tell Me Lies.
