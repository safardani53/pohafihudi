    Bradford Alexander Mehldau is an American jazz pianist, composer and composer. Mehldau studied music at New School, and toured and recorded while still a student. He was a member of the saxophonist Joshua Redman Quartet with bassist Christian McBride and drummer Brian Blade in the mid-1990s, and has commanded his own trio since the early 1990s. he includes bassist Larry Grenadier and drummer Jorge Rossy; in 2005, Jeff Ballard replaced Rossy. These groups have released dozens of albums under the name of pianist. Since the early 2000s, Mehldau has been experimenting with musical formats other than the trio and the solo piano.
