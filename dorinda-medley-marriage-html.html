    The Real Housewives of New York star Dorinda Medley has made it known that wedding bells are on the horizon.

    Medley, who has been married twice already, recently confirmed the possibility of yet another marriage by the end of the year. 

    That being said, Medley has spilled very little on who the groom would be. 

    Dorinda Medley's Third Marriage?

    Over an exclusive interview with Page Six, the reality star touched upon how she was just dipping her toes back in the dating pool. 

    This aligns with the interview she had over on SiriusXM back in December 2021. At the time, she disclosed she had begun dating someone new. 

    Medley had only been on three dates at the time of the interview, but as recent statements suggest, the dates must have turned into a dependable relationship. 

    Reeling back to the Page Six discussion, Medley shared she had decided to change things for 2022 on 2-22-2022. 

    The changes included getting a new apartment in a new area and possibly a new marriage. 

    "I just decided this [on] 2/22/2022, I really decided to change, it's all about change. I'm getting myself a new apartment. I'm going to move into a different area," she said.

    "And I really think I will be married by the end of 2022!"

    Dorinda Medley Wants a Partner in Life

    Professing further on her statement, Medley dished how the thought of a new marriage stemmed from the time she spent quarantining during the COVID-19 pandemic. 

    "It's not fun to be alone all the time," she acknowledged. "That may sound weak." 

    It wasn't that she didn't have friends and family that she could spend time with. In fact, it was quite the opposite. 

    However, she had concluded that she was ready to come back home to a loving partner, likely a husband. 

    While it is likely, the man she is considering marriage with was the same man she was talking about back in December. There has been no such confirmation as an official statement in the public sphere.

    With her first husband, Ralph Lynch, Medley is a mother to an adult daughter, Hannah. 

    She got married to her second husband, a hedge fund manager Richard Medley, from October 2005 until he died at age sixty in November 2011. 

    "I've been married and then widowed. I just really thought, 'That's it for me. That part of my life is over, enough is enough," she confessed. 

    Then again, in recent times, she admitted she was ready to explore new avenues.

    "I think I've got one more in me. I might be a three-timer!" she said of her possible third marriage. 

    What Dorinda Medley Is Looking for

    There are a few particular character traits that Medley has set for possible suitors. 

    "I need someone that makes me laugh. I love to have a fun time. I need someone that's kind," she said. "I just want someone that is going to be a partner in life."

    Albeit, this special someone need not be willing to share Medley's reality TV exploits.
