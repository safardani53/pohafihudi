    Kylie Jenner & Lil Twist Catch A Movie Together

    The two stars were spotted out heading into a theater in Calabasas, Calif. on July 23, according to Just Jared. They were both dressed for the summer and clearly enjoying each other’s company, smiling and laughing the whole way in.

    Earlier in the day Kylie tweeted, “I wanna see the conjuring so bad, was it good?” Looks like she was in the mood for a horror flick!

    Going to a scary movie is usually such a good date for a couple — so were Kylie and Twist out as more than friends?

    Kylie Jenner & Lil Twist: Are They Getting Romantic?

    It’s unlikely, at least according to Kylie herself. The last time she went on a cutesy one-on-one date with Lil Twist, stoking rumors by grabbing ice cream with him on July 3, she later responded strongly denying all claims that she and Twist were hooking up.

    “Why am I dating every male I hang out with I don’t understand,” Kylie tweeted. “Media always making it seem like I’m dating everyone I’m seen with.. Makes no sense to me. Don’t listen to everything u see, people.”

    Kylie’s right — she ought to be able to go out with a guy friend and not face rumors every single time. But really, couldn’t she and Lil Twist choose less romantic dates than seeing a scary movie and getting ice cream?

    Kylie Jenner & Jaden Smith: Keeping Their Relationship Private

    Furthermore, Kylie likely chose to go to the movies with Lil Twist instead of Jaden because she and her After Earth boyfriend are trying to keep their relationship private. A source close to Jaden, 15, explained that strategy to HollywoodLife.com exclusively:

    Jaden has seen what happens to relationships in the public eye. His parents [Will and Jada Pinkett Smith] are always being talked about getting divorced, cheating on each other or something else and his good friend Justin [Bieber] had to deal with his relationship with Selena [Gomez] constantly in the limelight as well. Add with what Kylie has seen her family go through, they both have decided to make things as normal as possible and not broadcast their relationship to the world.

    Apparently that means she has to see some movies with a friend instead of him.

    But what do you think, HollywoodLifers? Is something more going on with Kylie and Lil Twist? Let us know!
