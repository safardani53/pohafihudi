    Dancing With The Stars Finale 2023 LIVE DWTS winner reveal leaves fans saying 'well of course' -

    Even though Jason Mraz came in second place, he proved to have won fans over with his vulnerability and kindness. In a message posted toInstagram, he stood next to a poster of his partner and gushed over her.

    28 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Jason is the people's prince

    Even though Jason Mraz came in second place, he proved to have won fans over with his vulnerability and kindness.

    In a message posted to Instagram, he stood next to a poster of his partner and gushed over her.

    "Hey Daniella," the I'm Yours singer started. "No matter what happens, you're a winner. I’m so happy that I could assist in getting you to the finale yet again."

    After that, Jason started to get emotional, and he had to fight back tears.

    "I'm a crier, alright, and you made me cry. For a lot of reasons. For good and bad. I love you."

    More reactions from season 32 finale

    While some fans are thrilled for Xochitl Gomez, others are disappointed that their favorites didn't place higher.

    Other fans came after Xochitl because they thought she made a mistake in one of her final performances.

    One said: "Can we call out @carrieanninaba and the other judges for not calling out Xochitl on her mistake?

    "I’ve never watched before this season and I’d hate to find out that #DWTS is rigged."

    Another added: "Of course when Xochitl messes up the judges turn a blind eye. This finale is rigged."

    Fans claim 'Ariana was robbed'

    Fans of DWTS on X, formerly known as Twitter, ripped the show after Xochitl Gomez was announced as the winner.

    One said: "RIGGED SORRY ARIANA ROBBED."

    "Ariana deserved second place, she was robbed," said another.

    More fans said: "Jason and Daniella were robbed" and "Jason Mraz was robbed."

    A fifth wrote: "Just my opinion but Jason was given some tough choreo the whole season, was given difficult ballroom dances that other stars weren’t given (argentine tango), showed that when you’re knocked down to get back up again, AND always gave 110%… so yeah HE WON."

    Val Chmerkovskiy joins Mark Ballas with three wins

    Val Chmerkovskiy won his third Mirrorball Trophy on Tuesday's finale of Dancing With The Stars.

    Val won in 2015 when he took home the trophy with actress Rumer Willis for season 20.

    He won again the following year with Olympic gymnast Laurie Hernandez.

    Val now joins veteran pro Mark Ballas with three wins. They're both behind Derek Hough, who has a total of six trophies.

    Social media congratulates Xochitl!

    Social media users are congratulating Xochitl Gomez on winning the first-ever Len Goodman Mirrorball Trophy.

    One fan tweeted: "we're gonna look back one day when xochitl gomez has countless storage units filled with awards and be like 'remember when she was the first len goodman mirrorball trophy winner.'"

    Another wrote: "XOCHITL GOMEZ, MIRRORBALL CHAMPION!! SHE HAD IT IN THE BAG SINCE THE BEGINNING!!"

    A third added: "PEOPLES PRINCESS XOCHITL GOMEZ FOR THE WIN SHE WILL ALWAYS BE THAT GIRL!!"

    Fans send love to Jason and Daniella

    Fans are sending love to Jason and Daniella after they placed second.

    One wrote on X (formerly Twitter): "My champs. I have absolutely loved watching these two every week. They have without a doubt become my all time favorite partnership.

    "I’ve never rooted for a team harder than them. They may not have won in the end, but their partnership will forever be so special to me."

    Another wrote: "also one thing about daniella is she will always come out on top when it comes to freestyles like you can’t out do the out doer."

    "Dani has been in the finale 3/4 of her seasons. What a queen," a third added.
