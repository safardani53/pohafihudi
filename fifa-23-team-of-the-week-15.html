    FIFA 23s Team of the Week 15 has a Premier League goalie on top

    FIFA 23s fifteenth Team of the Week (TOTW) has boosted a Nottingham Forest player to 89. There are also two double-boosted cards this week that have an outstanding 88 rating. 1 Nottingham Forests goalie, Keylor Navas, is the top player this week with a rating of 89.

    11 Mar 2024, 00:00

    FIFA 23’s fifteenth Team of the Week (TOTW) has boosted a Nottingham Forest player to 89.

    There are also two double-boosted cards this week that have an outstanding 88 rating.

    Nottingham Forest’s goalie, Keylor Navas, is the top player this week with a rating of 89.

    There are no cards rated 90+, maintaining the recent trend with FIFA 23 TOTW.

    More in FIFA 23

    England’s Tammy Abraham is one of two double-boosted cards this week, with a six-point boost, from 82 to 88.

    The other is Barcelona’s Raphinha who has a smaller boost of five points, from 83 to 88.

    This is much lower than previous double-boosted cards that have seen boosts of up to 13 points.

    These are the highest-rated cards in FIFA Ultimate Team (FUT) this week, but there are also several other cards in the high 80s.

    Most read in Gaming

    If you download the FIFA 23 companion web or mobile app, you can open FIFA Ultimate Team (FUT) packs on the go.

    This means you can start building your team ready to play in the game.

    The TOTW cards will show up as special cards in FUT packs this week, with boosted stats.

    This week’s team will be available via FUT packs until Wednesday, February 15, 5pm UK time.

    After that, these boosted cards will only be purchasable via the transfer market, usually for high prices.

    Here is a list of all the players in FIFA 23’s Team of the Week and their boosted stats:

    • 89 - Navas
    • 88 - Coman, Abraham, Raphinha
    • 87 - Hakimi
    • 86 - Rúben Neves
    • 85 - Acerbi, Brandt, Laborda
    • 84 - Tarkowski, Rajković, Caio Henrique, João Mário, McGoldrick, Kolo Muani
    • 83 - Diallo, Pere Milla
    • 81 - Antwi-Anjei
    • 80 - Arslan
    • 79 - Albu, Van der Venne, Sepúlveda
