    PATSY Kensit looked unrecognisable as she treated herself to a spa day in London this week.

    The 49-year-old singer and actress revealed a dramatic transformation as she dressed down and went make-up free for the outing.

    Swapping her usual glamour for a baggy white anorak and tracksuit bottoms, Patsy covered her eyes with huge sunglasses outside, before relaxing with her eyes closed in the salon chair.

    Wearing her hair scraped back in a bun, and holding on to her phone in one hand, the star appeared relaxed as she strolled past cameras.

    The former Emmerdale actress appeared in a world of her own as she listened to some music after having her nails done.
