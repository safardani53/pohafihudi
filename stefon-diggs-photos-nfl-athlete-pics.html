    Stefon Diggs, born November 29, 1993, is an American football wide receiver for the Minnesota Vikings of the National Football League. He was drafted by the Vikings in the fifth round of the 2015 NFL Draft. Stefon, a Maryland native accepted an athletic scholarship to attend the University of Maryland in College Park, Maryland, where he played football from 2012 to 2014. He was selected second-team All-Big Ten in recognition of his successful junior season in 2014. Stefon has two younger brothers who play college football. In 2019, he collaborated with Adidas to co-design custom tracksuits with the apparel brand. He’s known for his unique sense of style and his sleek style and has been named one of the most stylish NFL players. He told HollywoodLife if he weren’t in the NFL he would be in the business of fashion, designing looks inspired by his own style. Stefon has a daughter who was born in 2016.
