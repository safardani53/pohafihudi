    LEFTY SM was a popular Mexican rapper with millions of social media followers.

    The 31-year-old was reportedly shot to death, and now fans want to know more about what happened.

    Who was rapper Lefty SM?

    Lefty SM was born Juan Carlos Sauceda in San Luis Río Colorado, Mexico, on April 22, 1992.

    He was a rapper signed by the label Alzada Records.

    Per MSN, he collaborated with well-known artists such as Luis R Conriquez, Carin Léon, Dharius, El Komander, and C-Kan.

    Lefty SM's song titled Soy Mexicano is "one of his biggest hits."

    Read More On Celebrity Deaths

    The music video for his song Mostaza — released on July 20, 2023 — has gained 1million views.

    The rapper was popular on social media, with 2.7million subscribers to his YouTube Channel, @LEFTYSMOFICIAL; 1.5million followers on his Facebook page, @LeftySMOficial; and 1.3million followers on his Instagram account, @leftysm_.

    How did Lefty SM die?

    On Sunday, September 3, 2023, Billboard reported that Lefty SM was shot to death in Zapopan, Jalisco in Mexico at the age of 31.

    Per reports, he was attacked near his home in the neighborhood of La Cima in Zapopan.

    Most read in Celebrity

    In a September 3 Instagram post, Alzada Records confirmed the artist's tragic death.

    According to Billboard, the English translation of the post reads: "Dear Alzada friends and family, with profound sadness we’re informing you about the death of our brother, Lefty SM, Juan Carlos Sauceda.

    "Our love and prayers are with his wife María Isabel and their two daughters."

    Carlos Félix, a PR rep for Alzada Records, told Billboard Español that the rapper was shot twice and declared dead at a local hospital.

    Did Lefty SM have any children?

    According to Alzada Records' statement, Lefty SM shared two daughters with his wife, María Isabel.

    There is little public information on his marriage and kids.
