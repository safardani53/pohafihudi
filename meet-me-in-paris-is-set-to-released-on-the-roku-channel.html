    Meet Me in Paris, billed as the first-of-its-kind reality rom-com, will be available to Roku subscribers on Valentine’s Day. The story will follow three real-life women, Danielle Matteo, Mimi Shou, and Jasmine Fleet, as they compose their screenplays for the film. The Roku channel romance, conceived as a reality television program, is set to launch in February on the platform. A teaser for the film has also been published, which previews what we might anticipate from it.

    Meet Me in Paris will be a touching and unique show.

    Here’s all you need to know about Meet Me in Paris, the new Roku channel romance.

    Meet Me in Paris movie release date and how to watch it in 2023

    The film will be released on the Roku streaming platform on February 10, 2023. Viewers may watch the film for free on Roku devices, the Web, iOS and Android smartphones, Amazon Fire TV, and certain Samsung TVs through the Roku channel. There is no need for a membership to view the movie on Roku.

    What exactly is the plot? The official summary and trailer have been released.

    Meet Me in Paris is a first-of-its-kind unscripted rom-com film produced by Reese Witherspoon’s Hello Sunshine and Zoe Saldana’s Cinestar. The 90-minute unscripted film recounts the tale of three single friends going on a once-in-a-lifetime trip to Paris. They’ve been looking for genuine love for two weeks. Will their first dates, which included meetings in Luxembourg Gardens and strolls along the picturesque Champs-Élysées, result in a happily ever after, or will they end in heartbreaks? According to the Roku channel, the official summary of the film is as follows:

    “Three actual ladies go to the City of Love in this brand new Roku Original reality film, where they pursue love much like in the movies. The ladies go about Paris, meeting guys, going on dates, and hunting for l’amour vrai.”

    The unscripted love drama’s executive producers include Reese Witherspoon, Zo Saldaa, Sara Rea, Sue Kinkead, Mariel Saldaa, Cisely Saldaa, and Angela Rae Berg. View the trailer here. The trailer starts with,

    “Hello and welcome to Paris… Where you’ve taken center stage as the star of your own romantic comedy.”

    The corny rom-com teaser follows three genuine ladies in a reality television-style setting as they go on dates, meet guys, and have fun in Paris. The film is not written and is based on a reality show premise.

    The Meet Me in Paris cast Explore

    Meet Me in Paris stars Danielle Matteo, a Basketball Beauties team member and the manager of a Los Angeles-based clothes boutique. She also worked at MVMT as an influencer marketing expert and as a reseller of watches, eyewear, and luxury accessories.

    Mimi Shou, an Instagram influencer who grew to prominence for opening a shop under her brand aimed at young ladies who are self-assured in their looks, has also been cast in the Roku romance. Jasmine Fleet, who performed in the Miss Universe 2023 contest in New Orleans, Louisiana, will join them in January.
