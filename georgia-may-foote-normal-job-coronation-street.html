    CORONATION Street's Georgia May Foote has a very normal job after leaving Weatherfield in 2015.

    The actress, who played Katy Armstrong in the ITV soap for five years, is now a very successful nail technician.

    Georgia qualified as one back in 2021 and launched her own business GMF Nails.

    The former soap star's salon is based in Middleton, Manchester, and offers a variety of different services such as builder gel, gel polish and nail art.

    Georgia shares snaps of her incredible designs on her Instagram page which boasts 5k followers.

    She's now running nail tutorials for qualified technicians.
