    Selena Gomez On Justin Bieber In Rehab — Leans On Demi Lovato

A source close to Selena tells HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY that Selena thinks that Justin is a mess, and that’s she’s already told him to go to rehab!

    Selena Wants Justin In Rehab After DUI

That is why she has been hanging out with Demi Lovato a lot lately, to get advice from her on how he should move forward in recovery.”

    Hopefully Justin listens to Selena’s advice — and if she doesn’t, at least she has her best girl friend to lean on for support!

    Justin’s Mom Wants To Stage An Intervention

    “Pattie is livid with what happened to her son under his father’s watch in South Beach,” a source tells HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY. “She’s been talking to specialists and others in Justin’s support network to strategize the best plan of action. She wants to stage an intervention, but she needs everyone onboard, and this includes Jeremy.”
