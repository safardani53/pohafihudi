    ZOFLORA'S limited edition Christmas scents dropped in stores this week - but cleaning fans have been left gutted after struggling to get their hands on them.

    Shoppers were eagerly awaiting the launch of the cleaning giant's Winter Spice and Cranberry & Orange range to kickstart the festive season.

    Zoflora bosses teased their release on social media last week as they said: "Let's get seasonal.

    "Spruce up your home with the return of two favourite seasonal scents."

    The cleaning giant claims their expert perfumers have "captured the essence of winter perfectly" with the fan-favourite items.

    Both scents were set to arrive in B&M, Home Bargains and Asda on Wednesday.

    And while the Orange and Cranberry scent has been spotted in stores, cleaning fans appear to have cleaned out the Winter Spice already.

    One disappointed shopper said: "There's no Winter Spice anywhere.

    "I tried two B&Ms - East Kilbride and Darnley - and Home Bargains at Auldhouse Retail Park in Glasgow, and East Kilbride.

    "A B&M worker told me they've not had any Winter Spice at all.

    Most read in Fabulous

    "I really wanted to get both scents because I know how quickly they sell."

    Another cleaning fan asked: "Which shop will I find the winter spice please?"

    And a third wrote: "What shop is selling them as not in B&M or Home Bargains today?"

    But if you're desperate to get your hands on the Christmas scent, don't worry - there's still time.

    The two winter scents will be available in stores until December 19, with new stock expected to keep up with demand.

    The Cranberry and Orange scent was inspired by a complex spice cocktail with the addition of "some Christmas spirit and fresh rhubarb".

    And Zoflora bosses describe their Winter Spice creation as a warm wintery fragrance blending juicy orange and peppermint on top, followed by a spicy heart of cinnamon, clove, and gingerbread on a sweet woody ambery background

    Shoppers can also pick up two other Christmas scents - with Winter Morning exclusively available in Home Bargains, while Vanilla and Amber has been spotted on shelves in B&M.
