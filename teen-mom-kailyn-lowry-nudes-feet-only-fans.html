    TEEN Mom star Kailyn Lowry revealed she plans to share nudes on her OnlyFans page, just days after saying she wouldnt post explicit photos. Fans slammed the 28-year-old reality star for charging $20 for a subscription to her account on the paid content service.

    11 Aug 2024, 00:00

    TEEN Mom star Kailyn Lowry revealed she plans to share “nudes” on her OnlyFans page, just days after saying she wouldn’t post “explicit” photos.

    Fans slammed the 28-year-old reality star for charging $20 for a subscription to her account on the paid content service.

    They originally felt the price was too high no matter what Kailyn planned to post but especially if she only shared the behind-the-scenes footage, as originally planned.

    On the latest episode of her Coffee Convos podcast, the mother of four announced she’s adding to her OnlyFans lineup.

    She told co-host Lindsie Chrisley: “I made an OnlyFans! It’s like a private Instagram that people pay for.

    “You make money on it… You post nudes and send feet pics.”

    After Lindsie asked if she planned on sending those types of videos and photos, Kailyn explained that that’s how people make most of their money on the platform, with someone she knows making $100 “for her feet pics.”

    The Teen Mom added that she’s “for sure gonna send feet pics,” as well as nude photos.

    Fellow Teen Mom Farrah Abraham is also on OnlyFans, and she recently updated her list to include “doggy style” and “golden showe” videos for $100 each.

    Before Kailyn posts any sexual snapshots, though, she plans to have her co-host approve each picture in order to not have “another horse pic situation happen ever again.”

    Kailyn continued: “I never want a photographer to give me this idea and tell me it’s amazing and have me post it and I’m trusting this photographer. 

    “I never want to do that again.”

    Reddit fans slammed the mother of four – who welcomed son Creed, her fourth child and second with ex Chris Lopez, last month – for “thinking she’s worth more than she actually is” and charging $20, while Farrah’s is only $7.99.

    They also blasted her for joining the platform just weeks after giving birth to her new baby and already getting ready to “sell nudes.”

    One user wrote: “Not surprising. This b***h is full of audacity. 

    “I would say I hope no one pays this but if you’re that stupid then that’s your fault.”

    One added that any of her free time should be spent on “self care” in order to keep her healthy for her kids, “not self promoting to fill an endless void, I am sad for her babies really.”

    The suggestion that Kailyn needs to take time for a little relaxation comes as she’s dealing with trolls who are suggesting Chris isn’t the father of her newborn because of Creed’s “skin color.”

    He recently slammed fans for making the accusation, and she followed it up with her own post, begging people to “stop commenting” about the paternity of her child.
