    GOOD Morning America’s Amy Robach has slipped and revealed an intimate detail about TJ Holmes before their romance was exposed.

    The two co-hosts, who are both married, are reported to have been having an affair over the last few months.

    During a November 16 episode of GMA, 49-year-old Amy appeared to let slip a clue about her romance with TJ, 45.

    During a segment featuring Softies robes, the presenter was admiring the texture and let slip: “I wish TJ was here. He loves his robes.”

    She then seemed to catch herself and backtracked: “He walks around the hallways with them.”

    Amy’s comment now seems much more significant after her and TJ’s secret rendezvous was exposed by DailyMail.com.

    Read more Amy and TJ

    The outlet has obtained videos and pictures of the pair on a secret weekend getaway, cozying up at an Irish bar, and getting an Uber to his Manhattan apartment.

    Amy is married to former Melrose Place actor Andrew Shue, 55.

    Meanwhile, TJ is married to attorney Marilee Fiebig, 44, with whom he shares a daughter.

    Both couples have been married for 12 years and both are reported to have split in August amid the affairs.

    MORE THAN FRIENDS

    An insider told DailyMail.com: "Everyone knows that Amy and TJ have been close friends for a long time now, running together and even socializing as a foursome with each other's spouses."

    The source claimed Amy and TJ's alleged affair started in June “when they were in London together filming the Queen’s Platinum Jubilee.”

    “Staff were buzzing about the intimacy between them.”

    Amy and TJ, who have been anchoring GMA 3 together since 2020, are known among viewers for their on-screen chemistry.

    They struck up a friendship outside of work as they bonded over their love of running and fitness.

    Just this month, they ran the New York City Marathon together and Amy shared photos of them crossing the finish line side by side.

    SIGNS OF THE SPLIT

    However, Amy’s “robe” comment was not the first clue that things were going downhill in her marriage.

    She has not shared a photo with Andrew on Instagram since July when she posted a selfie of them posing at sunset in Athens, Greece.

    Later that month, she shared a flirty photo of her with her co-host instead and wrote in the caption: “TJ just loves it when we match.”

    The TV personality has also restricted comments on her Instagram posts, seemingly after fans began noticing that she had stopped wearing her wedding ring.

    The U.S. Sun has contacted representatives for Amy and TJ for comment, but they have not yet responded.
