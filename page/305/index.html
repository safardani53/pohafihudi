CBS News Correspondent Richard Wagner Dead at 85

11 Feb 2024, 00:00

Richard Wagner, a longtime correspondent for CBS News, has died. He was 85. Wagner's wife Donna Lewis-Wagner confirmed the news of his death to PEOPLE, saying that her husband died at his home in Charlottesville, Virginia. A cause of death was not provided. Wagner was known for being a key contributor on CBS Evening News from the 1960s to the 1980s. The news reporter was assigned to Saigon in 1964, where he covered the Vietnam War at a time when correspondents were not yet accustomed to providing firsthand accounts of wartime activity to a television audience back home.
