    Dame Julia Mary Walters, known professionally as Julie Walters, is an English actress, comedian and author. She has received four British Academy Television Awards, two British Academy Film Awards, two international Emmy Awards, a BAFTA Scholarship and a Golden Globe Award. Walters has been nominated for an Academy Award twice, once for Best Actress and once for Best Supporting Actress. Walters became famous when starring in Education Rita, a role she created in the West End. She has appeared in a number of movies including Personal Service, Step Out, My Sister, Billy Elliot, Harry Potter series like Molly Weasley, Calendar Girls, Wah-Wah, Driving Lessons, Becoming Jane, Mamma Mia ! and its 2018 sequel, Brave, Paddington and its 2017 sequel, Brooklyn, Film Stars Don't Die in Liverpool, and Mary Poppins Returns. On stage, she won the Olivier Award for Best Actress for the 2001 movie All My Sons
