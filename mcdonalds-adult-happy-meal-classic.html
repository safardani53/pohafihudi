    I tried McDonald's adult Happy Meal - it may sound childish but the classic has a new twist

    MCDONALD'S, where a kid can be a kid, and where adults can be one too. The chain is now offering an adult Happy Meal called The Cactus Plant Flea Market Box, giving customers a chance to turn back time.

    09 Jun 2024, 00:00

    MCDONALD'S, where a kid can be a kid, and where adults can be one too.

    The chain is now offering an adult Happy Meal called The Cactus Plant Flea Market Box, giving customers a chance to turn back time.

    The meals are part of a special limited-edition collaboration between McDonald's and clothing brand Cactus Plant Flea Market.

    From October 3 to October 30, McDonald's lovers can choose from two options: the Big Mac Combo Meal or the 10-piece Chicken McNuggets Meal.

    Both meals come with a drink, fries, and a mystery collectible.

    What's a Happy Meal without a toy? Well, collectible.

    Read More on McDonald's

    The collectibles, or figurines, come in four different options three of which are takes on classic McDonald's characters Grimace, Hamburglar and Birdie the Early Bird.

    The fourth figurine is a yellow collectible with a McDonald's shirt called Cactus Buddy.

    The outside of the box is brightly colored and features the famous golden arches.

    The review

    The first question I asked myself while I was waiting in the expected lengthy line was, is this worth it?

    Most read in Money

    The answer - it depends.

    If you like nostalgia then this is for you.

    I chose the Big Mac, my favorite, and since I live in California, I can get a Big Mac combo for a little over $10 while the adult Happy Meal costs $12.38.

    So for two more dollars, you'd get a toy and the classic Happy Meal pack.

    As I pulled up to pay, I asked the cashier if many have purchased the new meal, and to my surprise, she told me the three people in front of me ordered them.

    She expected the day would be filled with customers enjoying a stroll down memory lane.

    What is Cactus Plant Flea Market?

    Cactus Plant Flea Market is a brand of graphic t-shirts, sweatshirts, hats, and jackets spotted on major celebrities like Pharell, Kanye West, Travis Scott, and Offset.

    The resell value of the products is incredibly high as some sweatshirts have resold for over $800.

    The clothes are normally hard to get your hands on as it's available in boutiques with extremely limited supplies.

    Customers can order through the app and be entered to win the gear.

    The Cactus Plant Flea Market and McDonald's merch can be purchased directly from McDonald's website at 11am EST on October 3.

    The merch will reportedly include a t-shirt, a hoodie, a Grimace chair, and a Cactus Plant Flea Market x McDonald's acrylic sign.
