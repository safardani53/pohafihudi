    Geordie Shore's Sophie Kasaei moves back to Newcastle after split from Joel Corry

    SOPHIE Kasaei heaves heavy boxes as she moves back in with her father following her split from her boyfriend. The newly slim Geordie Shore star, 28, is back up north in Newcastle after splitting with boyfriend of five years Joel Corry.

    23 Feb 2024, 00:00

    SOPHIE Kasaei heaves heavy boxes as she moves back in with her father following her split from her boyfriend.

    The newly slim Geordie Shore star, 28, is back up north in Newcastle after splitting with boyfriend of five years Joel Corry.

    The star, who has lost an incredible two stone thanks to a gruelling exercise and diet regime, looked in good spirits as she moved boxes full of her belongings into her dad's home - and even giggled as one box full of shoes fell open on the ground.

    The brunette looked toned in a pair of skintight leggings as she showed off the results of her incredible weight loss.

    She added a splash of colour with a cropped white and yellow jumper and wore black trainers for her errands.

    She has moved back in with her father after giving up life down south.

    Sophie split from Joel Corry last September and was living in Essex with her cousin Marnie Simpson.

    However Sophie missed her Newcastle roots so wanted to be back in her home town of South Shields.

    After losing two stone Sophie said: “I look and feel the best I ever have, we start filming for Geordie Shore again next week and now that I’m single, things will be interesting as I’ve never been single on the show.

    "I’m really looking forward to it and who knows what might happen.”

    The Sun Online broke the news that Sophie split from boyfriend Joel after five years of dating.

    The reality star sparked speculation it was over at the MTV awards when she refused to comment on her relationship status.

    She said: “It’s just complicated, it’s complicated you know.”

    In a social media post, she wrote: “Right I think I'm officially getting back to normal now after filming, starting to feel slightly human again. I'm ready for new beginnings.”

    Sophie and Joel first met when he was working as a production intern at MTV and they started dating in 2012.

    He joined her on the show in the second series and the couple were plagued with drama continuously, amid ongoing cheating allegations.
