    WWE legend Trish Stratus stole the show at WrestleMania 39 with a sensational outfit on her return match.

    However, the Canadian grappler also had all eyes on her thanks to her busty attire.

    Stratus donned a short white shirt, leather tights, suspenders and a black cowboy hat.
