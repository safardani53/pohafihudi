    Aayushmaan Srivastav Age & Early Life

    Aayushmaan Srivastav, was born in Mumbai amid his father’s difficult times. He revealed his birth year as 1996-1997. While growing up in a loving family with his father, he had the chance to attend many schools. He originally attended the Narsee Monjee Educational Trust – Jamnabai Narsee Institution, a well-known school in Mumbai. He then transferred to Mumbai’s Oberoi International School.     Career

    While finishing his schooling, Aayushmaan Srivastav made music a part of his life. He started studying the Sitar, a musical instrument. As a specialist in Sitar, he added the sound of his Sitar to numerous musical works. In 2019, he was spotted supporting artist Kailash Kher’s campaign, for which he played the Sitar.

    On the happiest occasion of Guru Purnima the same year, he performed Sitar alongside the eminent Indian celebrity Baba Pt. Kartick Kumar. In 2020, he will be recognized in the Guinness World Records as a professional Sitar player. He performed tunes from the Amazon Prime Series “The Forgotten Army” with 1046 other artists. The combined efforts of the 1046 musicians, including Aayushmaan, established a new record for the biggest cinematic music ensemble.

    Aayushmaan received an accolade from the Governor of Maharashtra for playing for him at a government function only a few months before his father’s death. On August 26, 2021, he shared photos of himself making a cameo in a short film created by his sister Antara Srivastav.
