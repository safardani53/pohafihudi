    DECLAN Donnelly serenaded New Year's Eve punters with a dazzling rendition of Billy Joel's Piano Man.

    The I'm a Celeb star, 48, belted out the track at a boozer near Newcastle as regulars rang in 2024.

    Dec then invited locals back to his mum's home nearby as the celebrations continued.

    Footage showed the TV star in front of the mic at The Bagder in posh Ponteland, Northumberland.

    He joined local band, The Optics, for the impromptu singalong of the 1973 ballad.

    The crowd cheered as he looked at the lyrics on his phone and sang: "Now John at the bar is a friend of mine...He gets me my drinks for free..."
