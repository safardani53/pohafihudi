    FANS have predicted the top three singers of The Voice ahead of the upcoming finale. In anonline thread, viewers have picked out Ruby Leigh, 16, Michael Huntley, 33, and Nini Iris, 27, as the top contenders.

    21 Aug 2024, 00:00

    FANS have predicted the top three singers of The Voice ahead of the upcoming finale.

    In an online thread, viewers have picked out Ruby Leigh, 16, Michael Huntley, 33, and Nini Iris, 27, as the top contenders.

    One fan praised: "Huntley was a given. 

    "Nini consistent, star power, amazing vocals. Commanding. Likable."

    Another fan mentioned: "I think [John Legend] has talent on his team for sure, but I think if you put any of them up against Nini, Ruby, or Huntley, I just don’t think they have much of a chance." 

    A third fan theorized: "Huntley is also very popular. I could see the final 3 being Ruby, Huntley & Nini, with either Ruby or Huntley winning."

    More On The Voice

    A fourth person singled out: "Ruby's a phenomenon."

    A commentator chimed: "I don’t think [Mac Royals] will win against them. 

    "You can tell the judges are really pushing Nini, Ruby, and Huntley."

    The Season 24 finale of The Voice airs Tuesday, December 19 on NBC.

    Most read in Entertainment

    NEW CHANGES AHEAD?

    The predictions came as critics have been demanding a new roster change.

    The commotion started when Gwen Stefani, 54, returned to the Voice in Season 24 after leaving her coach role in Season 20.

    During the latest season, the former No Doubt singer reunited with the other coaches, John, 44, and Niall Horan, 30.

    Reba McEntire, 68, made her debut on the program and filled in the empty seat left by Gwen's husband, Blake Shelton, 47. 

    Followers have urged production to fire Gwen and replace her with singer Kelly Clarkson, 41.

    The daytime TV show host was a past coach on the show during seasons 14 through 21.

    NEW COACH?

    One critic did not hold back on X, formerly known as Twitter, and tweeted: "I hate Gwen Stefani. I miss Kelly Clarkson."

    A second critic agreed: "Me too! I loved Kelly and I kinda don't wanna watch anymore. Gwen is such a disappointment."

    A third one stated: "WHAT DO WE HAVE TO DO TO GET KELLY BACK? Gwen has got to go."

    A fourth viewer stated: "Please bring Kelly back."

    Another viewer exclaimed: "I love Gwen, but she sucks at being a judge. Why do they keep inviting her? Kelly, where are you?!"

    During a recent episode, viewers watched as the judges were forced to pit two of their team members against one another.

    Although the other judges had difficulty making a decision, Gwen, in particular, struggled the most as she appeared to have a hard time choosing who she wanted to dub victorious.
