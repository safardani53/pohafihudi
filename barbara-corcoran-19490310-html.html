    Barbara Ann Corcoran is an American businesswoman, investor, speaker, consultant, columnist, author and television personality. She founded The Corcoran Group, a New York-based real estate broker, which she sold to NRT for $ 66 million in 2001. Corcoran, one of the Chapter's first “Shark” investors, has appeared in All 12 Seasons of ABC's Shark Tanks to date. In February 2020, she completed 53 trades on the show, the largest of which was a $ 350,000 investment for 40% of Coverplay. Corcoran is a columnist for More, The Daily Review and Redbook. She has written several books and has been featured on Larry King Live, NBC's Today show, hosted Millionaire Broker with Barbara Corcoran on CNBC, wrote a weekly column on New York Daily News, and is a speaker and consultant.
