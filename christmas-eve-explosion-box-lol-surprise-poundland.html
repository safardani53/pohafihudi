    AS we're sure all parents will agree, there's NOTHING that will stop our little ones from bouncing off the walls on Christmas Eve - except perhaps an explosion box filled to the brim with festive goodies.

    Well if you're looking to treat your LOL Surprise-obsessed children the night before Christmas, then one mum's budget-friendly explosion box may be about to come to the rescue - and it might just bag you a moment's peace in the process.

    Mum-of-two Rhian De la Mare created an incredible LOL Surprise-themed Christmas Eve explosion box using Poundland and Primark bargains.

    In total, the mum spent £15 putting together the festive treat for her 8-year-old daughter - which included stickers, badges, Primark chocolate lollipops and instant hot chocolate sachets.

    What's more, Rhian even picked up the exploding box in Poundland - meaning she didn't have to faff around making her own.

    Sharing a photo of her creation on the Poundland Appreciation Society Facebook page, the mum wrote: "My attempt at the exploding boxes. Think my daughter is going to love it."

    Unsurprisingly, other members of the group were similarly impressed - and questioned Rhian on how she put it all together.

    One replied: "Wow this is amazing, wish I had little girls."

    Another added: "This is wicked! By the way, in Home Bargains they have these lipsticks that are actual sweets - really cheap and cute."

    "I might have to try and make one," a third gushed.
