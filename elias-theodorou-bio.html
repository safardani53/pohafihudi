    Elias Theodorou | Career

    Before winning the Ultimate Fighter Nations Finale and gaining his fame, Elias had fought eight times professionally.

    Impressively, Theodorou defeated all his opponents to amass an undefeated record of eight wins and no losses.

    As a result, the UFC invited the talented Candian fighter to compete in the Ultimate Fighter Nations: Canada vs. Australia.

    There, under the tutelage of head coach Patrick Cote, Elias defeated all his opponents to win the tournament.

    Consequently, Theodorou earned a lucrative multiple-fight contract with UFC. After that, he won two more bouts to take his record of 11-0 before registering the first loss of his career.

    To be precise, Elias faced his toughest opponent to date, Thiago Santos, at UFC Fight Night: Namajunas vs. Van Zant.

    But, sadly, Theodorou got completely outclassed as Thiago won the bout via a unanimous decision.

    Thereafter, Theodorou fought against six more opponents from 2016 till 2018. In those matches, Elias recorded five victories and a sole loss against Brad Tavares.

    And, similar to his last defeat, Theodorou lost the bout via a unanimous decision.

    Elias Theodorou vs. Derek Brunson

    By the time UFC Fight Night: Iaquinta vs. Cowboy came, Elias was on a high as he was on a three-fight win streak.

    On the other hand, his opponent Derek Brunson was coming into the fight on two consecutive losses.

    Hence, all bets were on Elias to continue his impressive run and beat Derek.

    Contrarily, Brunson dominated his opponent throughout the duration of the three rounds. As a result, he won the fight through a unanimous decision.

    Released from UFC

    Sadly, just after losing to Derek, on May 26, 2019, it was announced that Elias was let go by the UFC as his contract had expired.

    Even though the fighter had an impressive record of eight wins and three losses in his 11 fights in UFC, it was not enough to impress Dana White, the UFC president.

    Post UFC Career

    After his release from the UFC, Elias fought at PFC 12 event on December 6, 2019. Moreover, Theodorou fought Hernani Perpetuo, whom he dispatched via punches in the third round’s 1 minute 51-second mark.

    Other than that, Elias also became the first MMA fighter to receive an exemption for medical cannabis (TUE) by the British Columbia Athletic Commission.
