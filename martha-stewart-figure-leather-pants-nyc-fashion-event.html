    MARTHA Stewart has shown off an incredible figure at a prestigious fashion event in New York.

    The longtime lifestyle expert donned tight leather pants and sky-high platform heels at The 92nd Street Y event.

    Martha, 82, glammed up as she strutted across the red carpet of the nonprofit community and cultural center.

    The Living Omnimedia founder donned skintight pants as she walked across in beige platform sandals.

    The cookbook author stunned in a business-like blazer over her silver blouse.

    Martha flaunted her long blond locks before heading off onto the Upper East Side stage with moderator Fern Mallis.

    READ MORE on Martha Stewart

    'A REMARKABLE CAREER'

    Back in June, to kick off the 150th season of 92NY, Fern announced a longtime writer as part of the fall roster for her Fashion Icons series.

    The Fashion Icons creator told Women's Wear Daily: “I first worked with her on an event, when she was just a caterer in Westport, Connecticut, and I had a PR business in the city.

    “It’s a remarkable career and I can’t think of anyone else like her.”

    The chat also included the topic of the retail businesswoman making the cover of the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit issue.

    Most read in Celebrity

    THE COVER MODEL

    In May, the 81-year-old star became the oldest model to land on the sports magazine. 

    She told the publication: "When I heard that I was going to be on the cover of Sports Illustrated Swimsuit, I thought, 'Oh, that's pretty good, I'm going to be the oldest person I think ever on a cover of Sports Illustrated.'

    "And I don't think about age very much, but I thought that this is kind of historic."

    Martha continued: "Age is not the determining factor in terms of friendship or in terms of success, but what people do, how people think, how people act, that’s what’s important and not your age."

    For the steamy photo shoot of the cover, the media personality traveled to the Dominican Republic.

    Martha hit up the beach in 10 different swimwear looks, including a plunging red halter swimsuit, a tan one-piece with an oversized beach hat, and a metallic black-and-silver zip-up suit.

    In addition to the lifestyle guru, Jennifer's Body actress Megan Fox, 37, and model Brooks Nader, 26, will have their own cover for the 2023 SI Swimsuit Issue. 

    Martha was hitched to her ex-husband Andrew Stewart from 1961 to 1990 and has one daughter, 56-year-old Alexis Stewart.

    Alexis was married to John Cuti from 1997 to 2004, and she has two young children: Truman and Jude.
