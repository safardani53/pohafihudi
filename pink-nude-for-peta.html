    Pink: Nude for PETA!

    29 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Pink is donning her birthday suit for what she believes to be an important cause.

    The singer has gone naked once again for a PETA billboard, becoming the latest celebrity to make it very clear: she would rather bare it all than wear fur.

    “I’ve always felt that animals are the purest spirits in the world,” Pink tells Us Weekly. “They don’t fake or hide their feelings, and they are the most loyal creatures on Earth.”

    The caption of Pink’s large ad reads: “Be comfortable in your own skin, and let animals keep theirs.”

    The artist does admit, however, that she isn’t perfect. She has donned this evil material in the past.

    “I would like to say I’ve always been fur-free,” the Grammy winner explained.  “Unfortunately, I went through a selfish phase and wore fur on a couple of occasions. But I wised up and now boycott fur completely.

    “I wish everyone was forced to learn the horrors that these animals go through for fashion trends. I hope fur wearers get bitten in the ass by the same kind of animal they wear on their back.”

    Other big names who have posed in similar fashion for PETA include Olivia Munn, Wendy Williams and Khloe Kardashian.
