    SPENCER Matthews has shocked fans by sharing how often he and his wife Vogue Williams have sex. 

    The couple admitted on their popular podcast that despite being busy parents, they still manage to keep the spark alive in the bedroom.

    Speaking on Spencer & Vogue, the duo revealed that have had sex every night for the past few weeks - only missing two nights.

    Vogue, 37, told their listeners: “Couples are having sex less than ever before - 30% less than our parents."

    Spencer, 34, was quick to jump in and said: "Not in our house! Okay, I'm going to put something out there and it's going to upset a lot of parents."

    He continued: “You and I have sex all the time, ok, so listen to this - we have sex quite a lot, don’t we? We’re on a real hot streak.

    “The last 3 or 4 weeks - there’s probably only about 2 nights where we haven’t had sex."

    Vogue replied: “We’re on a hot streak - it doesn’t mean we’re always going to be on a hot streak."

    She then added: “I now regret the sex chat."

    Vogue previously expressed her desire to have four children by age 40, but the demands of life with three kids may mean it's no longer an option.

    "It was never a really rigid rule," she told The Sun. "I don't have an exact rule. People can have kids when they choose to have kids.

    "I just wanted them close together because I'm really close to my brothers, but saying that, it was kind of a shock, the third.

    "We're delighted now, but when you go from one to two you can kind of get yourself together a few weeks in, but when I went from two to three I did find it quite a big jump.

    "I was struggling to be able to organise my time. I thought I'll never get out the door ever again. it's so full on, and then you eventually sort it out."

    But if she does decide to add to their brood, then there's no 40 deadline.

    "Of course if you go beyond 40, so be it," said Vogue. "But in my mind I was thinking I'd love to have four kids and kind of be done when I was 40.

    "I don't know if we'll do four kids. They're really really tiring, and I thought I might be too tired, I kind of want to be chilling out then."

    Vogue and Spencer tied the knot in 2018.

    The pair had their first child, a son called Theodore in September 2018.

    In 2020 their daughter Gigi was born, with their second son Otto arriving in April 2022.
