    MACARONI MADNESS

    Popeyes is known for its authentic Louisiana-style fried chicken, but they also have a wide selection of sides.

    Mac & cheese cups are available to order online for $3.99, along with other options like Cajun rice for $3.50, slaw for $3.25, and mash and Cajun gravy for $2.99.

    Jazmynn shared a spicy revelation about the iconic cheesy comfort food and how it's made.
