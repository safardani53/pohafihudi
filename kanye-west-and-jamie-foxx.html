    Know About Kanye West and Jamie Foxx Relationship

    Kanye West has been in the news lately because of his struggles, his spat with Pete Davidson, and his new Netflix documentary, Jeen-Yuhs, all of which center on him. Celebrity pals, including comedian and singer Jamie Foxx, have been by Wests side.

    01 May 2024, 00:00

    Kanye West has been in the news lately because of his struggles, his spat with Pete Davidson, and his new Netflix documentary, Jeen-Yuhs, all of which center on him.

    Celebrity pals, including comedian and singer Jamie Foxx, have been by West’s side.

    Who Is Jamie Foxx?

    In 1989, Jamie Foxx began his stand-up comedy career by performing at local nightclubs. He made his cinematic debut in 1992 with the comedy Toys. He has been in some comedies and dramas since then, including Horrible Bosses, Django Unchained, and most recently, Ray.

    Slow Jamz, his first Billboard Top 100 hit single with Kanye West, was released in 2003, and it was then that he began dabbling in the music industry. Since then, numerous of Foxx’s tracks have been in the top 100. He earned an Academy Award for his performance as Ray Charles in the film Ray.

    Among his most recent credits are Disney’s Soul and Marvel’s Spider-Man: No Way Home, both of which were box office successes.

    When did Kanye West and Jamie Foxx first meet?

    The first time Kanye West and Jamie Foxx met was at a home party Foxx was holding in the early 2000s. “This kid walks in with a bag on, and his jaw is smashed,” said Jamie Foxx on The Graham Norton Show that night. The following night, Foxx requested a freestyle from the child, who turned out to be a young Kanye West. Foxx said this at the time: “Wow, this is impressive. You’ll become a household name.” Foxx and West recorded slow Jamz at his home studio in 2004. On his album College Dropout, Kanye West included the song.

    They reunited for the Grammy-winning song “Gold Digger” in 2005.

    Are they still in contact, or have they become estranged?

    It was reported on West’s Instagram account that they reunited on February 26, 2022. The two hadn’t been photographed together since 2017 before this Instagram image.

    When asked about a new album, they suggested that they could be working on something new together.

    Kanye West Wiki

    He (born Kanye Omari West; June 8, 1977) is an American rapper, record producer, and fashion designer, better known by his stage name Kanye West. West was born in Atlanta and reared in Chicago, where he developed the “chipmunk soul” sampling technique while working for Roc-A-Fella Records in the early 2000s. He released his debut studio album, The College Dropout, in 2004 to critical and commercial acclaim and formed the record label GOOD Music, which he continues to run today. He is recognized as one of the most important and well-respected artists of his generation in hip-hop and one of the most influential.
