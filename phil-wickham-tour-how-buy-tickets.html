    Phil Wickham tour: How can I buy tickets?

    07 Oct 2024, 00:00

    PHIL Wickham is a Contemporary Christian musician, singer, and songwriter who rose to fame in the early 2010s.

    In March 2022, he will embark on a cross country tour and now fans want to know how they can get their tickets.

    How can I buy tickets?

    Tickets for the Phil Wickham tour are on sale now with some locations already being sold out.

    Tickets can be purchased through the Ticketmaster website with prices starting at $29.50.

    Phil's tour also has a VIP option available for $79.95 which includes access to the best seats in the house and a live Q&A with the star prior to the show.

    Who is Phil Wickham?

    Phil Wickham is a musician who hails from San Diego, California.

    In 2006 he released his self-titled debut album which was an instant hit among fans.
