    Barry Williams is famous for his TV role on The Brady Bunch, but he settled down with someone outside of Hollywood’s spotlight. After getting married and divorced twice, Barry finally met the love of his love in 2016. Keep reading to find out everything we know about Barry’s wife! 

    Who Is Barry Williams’ Wife? 

    Barry is married to Tina Mahina, who is reportedly a professional hula dancer. Since the Mega Piranha actor is currently starring on season 32 of Dancing With the Stars, his wife most likely knows how the dance world can be with rigorous rehearsals and competitiveness.  

    How Long Have Barry Williams and His Wife Been Together? 

    Barry proposed to Tina in November 2016, which he revealed via social media at the time. 

    “[Four] minutes before this sunset photo was taken, I got down on one knee with a proposal,” he tweeted, adding, “She said ‘YES’!!!” 

    The following year, the couple got married in Missouri over the summer of 2017. To commemorate their second wedding anniversary, Barry shared a sweet photo to Instagram in July 2019, writing, “My lovely bride and I celebrated our 2nd wedding anniversary last evening at #chateauonthelake.” 

    From time to time, Barry gushes over his wife on social media. Among his most recent public shout-outs to the Tulsa, Oklahoma, native was in 2022 when he tweeted a stunning headshot of Tina. 

    “My lovely lady, Tina Mahina Williams,” he wrote at the time. “She’s fierce, and I love it!” 

    Who Are Barry Williams’ Ex-Wives? 

    Before he found love with Tina, Barry had been married twice in the past. His first marriage was with ex-wife Diane Martin from 1990 to 1992. 

    Barry’s second wife is Eila Mary Matt. The two tied the knot in 1999 but divorced in 2005. Following his second divorce, the California native was in a relationship with ex Elizabeth Kennedy until 2013. 

    How Many Kids Does Barry Williams Have? 

    The Mission: Impossible TV series alum is a dad to two children. Barry shares his first kid, son Brandon, born in 2003, with his second wife, Eila. The actor welcomed his second child, daughter Samantha Rose Williams, with his ex Elizabeth in 2012. 

    Though Barry rarely talks about his children in public, he opened up to Closer in 2021 about his relationship with his kids and their career endeavors. 

    “My son is a musician but also a full-time student and hasn’t made any [comments] about going into that as a profession,” the father of two told the outlet, referring to the possibility of Brandon pursuing a music career. As for Samantha, Barry told the outlet, “My little girl is a natural. She’s got a big personality, but I am not sure she would choose [entertainment] as a profession, either.”
