    5 Survivor Underdogs

    Spencer returned for his second chance at the game in Survivor: Cambodia (Season 31) where he came in third place. Watch all past seasons of Survivor on CBS All Access. Plus, don't miss the season premiere of Survivor: Game Changers on Wednesday, March 8 on CBS and CBS All Access.

    01 Feb 2024, 00:00
    Young lad Spencer quickly found himself in trouble during his first appearance on Survivor: Cagayan. He sunk to the bottom of the Brains tribe and later found himself in the minority following the merge. In true underdog style, Spencer fought hard to stay in the game, winning three Individual Immunity challenges and working closely enough with tribe kingpin—and Season 28 winner—Tony Vlachos to finish in a solid fourth place.

    Spencer returned for his second chance at the game in Survivor: Cambodia (Season 31) where he came in third place.
