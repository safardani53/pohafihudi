    Georgina, Andy, Jayne and Evelyn are the next Big Brother housemates up for eviction The Sun

    Georgina Leigh Cantwell, Andy West, Jayne Connery and Evelyn Ellis are the next round of housemates up for the public vote on Big Brother. The foursome will now face eviction from the house on Fridays live eviction.

    03 May 2024, 00:00

    Georgina Leigh Cantwell, Andy West, Jayne Connery and Evelyn Ellis are the next round of housemates up for the public vote on Big Brother.

    The foursome will now face eviction from the house on Friday’s live eviction.

    This round of nominations was the first time that all members of the house were able to nominate, with the first week being just the main housemates voting against each other, and the second week being the main housemates voting against the ‘Others’.

    The Valleys star Lateysha, who on Sunday had an explosive row with ex-journalist Andy, put him up for nomination as she said: “Andy…I feel like he’s sly, manipulative…and I feel like he tried to turn everyone against me.”

    Private investigator Jayne, who only last week tried to escape the house when the contestants were moved when the living room flooded, was put up for the chop by Georgina, who claimed she “swerved” last week’s nominations and was a “demon”.

    Chelsea Singh, who had a tempestuous relationship with Jayne before they entered the house, also put her up for nomination, and has made no secret of wanting her to leave.

    Georgina, who is currently in the middle of a budding romance with smitten Jackson Blyton, was put up by other housemates after becoming territorial over her new other half.

    When nominating her, Emma said: “Since they have got together I feel like Jackson doesn’t speak to me at all.  I would rather she wasn’t in the House.”

    The news will come as a devastating blow for Jackson, who has already told her that he loves her, and would even marry her if given the chance.

    Meanwhile, Aussie Evelyn, who has become a double act with Laura Carter since Marco Pierre White’s eviction, was put up for nomination after the fall out of Big Brother’s Mind Games task.

    Sam was left devastated after he was forced to watch footage of her and Laura refer to him as “Lateysha’s little b****”.

    Chelsea, Lateysha and Hughie Maughan all managed to evade the public vote- despite the housemates learning that their hot water had been shut off after the trio discussed nominations shortly before they nominated.

    In yesterday’s episode, after Hughie mistakenly reassured Lateysha they were allowed to speak about past nominations, she admitted she picked him and Natalie for the chop in last week’s vote.

    She said of her choice: ““I deeply regret my last nominations, I tried to go back in and change it.

    “That’s probably the biggest regret I’ve had since I’ve been in here.”

    The three then correctly predicted Jayne’s nomination, adding they all felt that she would “1000%” be put in the firing line.

    Who goes? You decide.
