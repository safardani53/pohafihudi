    EARLIER this week, 31-year-old Stephen Bear reunited with his teen girlfriend as they rekindled their romance following a split in November.

    He and Tia McAlister, who turned 18 earlier this month, originally started dating a year ago - and Bear even got a huge image of her face tattooed onto his back when she was just 17.

    Bear is heavily inked, and he has a huge portrait of three different women across his back and shoulders - with one holding a gun, another wearing a bandana across her mouth, and the third blindfolded.

    The Sun can reveal that Tia inspired the centre woman, who has her mouth covered and is wearing a baseball cap.

    A source previously shared the image of Tia that the star gave to his tattoo artist before he went under the needle, and it is clear that he was most inspired by her eyes.

    While the rest of the tattoo is in black, the tribute to Tia has a icy blue irises.

    It also has her same defined thick, dark eyebrows, although many of her other features are obscured by the etching's bandana.

    In addition to the huge tribute to Tia's face, Bear also got a matching tattoo of the Capricorn starsign with the beautician in August.

    And when they split in November, Bear began the process of getting the matching one removed - with a source previously telling us that this left Tia "angry and bitter".

    They said: "It came as a huge blow when they broke up, she was angry and bitter about the split because he was posting his new girl on his Instagram page and getting their tattoo removed.

    "They'd both got a Capricorn star sign on their hands at the end of August, and he also got an inking of her face on his back.

    "He only started to get the matching one removed, the back one is still there completely."

    The insider also told how Tia's concerned friends have warned her against dating Bear due to their 13-year age gap, but the teen hopes being linked to him will kickstart her career as an influencer.

    They told The Sun: "Several friends have tried to warn Tia against Bear due to the huge age gap, but because he's famous she sees this as an opportunity.

    "She is an aspiring influencer, so she'd use this as a way of getting in the limelight."

    Tia was 17 when she first started dating Bear, with our source saying she always remained "secretive" about how they met.

    However, they both regularly documented their fling on social media, with Tia enjoying time at his house over the summer.

    They split following a romantic holiday in October after Bear was unfaithful, but he reached out to the teen last month after his ex Georgia Harrison accused him of secretly filming them having sex, and the pair were quick to reconcile upon his recent return to the UK from Dubai.

    Bear and Tia were seen outside of his Essex home in his Mercedes, with an insider telling MailOnline at the time: "Tia met up with Bear the first opportunity she had after he was released from custody on Saturday and is standing by him amid the allegations."

    The Ex On The Beach star returned from Dubai, where he's been staying since before Christmas, on Friday and was arrested after touching down at Heathrow Airport.

    Essex Police quizzed him on allegations that he posted a sex video of his ex-girlfriend Georgia Harrison online after Bear was arrested on suspicion of disclosing private sexual images and obstructing a police officer.

    He has previously denied the revenge porn accusations, and was released on bail until February 10.
