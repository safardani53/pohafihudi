    Devin Armani Booker is an American professional basketball player who plays shooting guard for the Phoenix Suns of the National Basketball Association (NBA).

    Booker was born in Grand Rapids, Michigan on October 30, 1996. He started his NBA journey in 2015 after playing a season with the Kentucky Wildcats.

    He was drafted as the 13th overall pick by the Phoenix Suns and has been playing with the same franchise ever since.

    In 2017, aged 20, the NBA star player became the youngest player to score over 70 points in a game.

    In 2019, the shooting guard scored 59 points against Utah Jazz and 50 points against the Wizards and became the youngest player in NBA history with consecutive 50-point games.

    Booker is also involved in a romantic relationship with Kendall Jenner. They started dating in 2018 and split in June 2022, but reportedly, they are back together now.

    Does Devin Booker Follow Christian Faith?

    An interest constantly roams around Booker’s followers’ minds about what religion their favorite NBA player follows.

    To answer your curiosity, without a doubt, the Suns’ shooting guard is a Christian devotee.

    Coming from mixed ethnicity, the player celebrates Christmas as his major festival. He has followed the same spiritual path as his parents and grandparents.

    He is a believer of God. For every success he has achieved so far in his life, he attributes them all to the almighty. He also claims that whatever he does is the reflection of what God has given him.

    Devin Booker, the Phoenix Suns shooting guard, agreed, saying he relies on prayer throughout his career.

    “I wouldn’t be in this situation that I am without it,” he said. “I pray every day.”

    He is one of the NBA star players with a strong faith in God and his creations.

    Some sources also claim Booker to be Jewish, but he hasn’t mentioned him being Jewish. However, he has said that his godfather is Jewish.

    Devin Booker: Family Members

    The 27 -year-old NBA player is the son of Veronica Gutierrez, a cosmetologist, and Melvin Booker, a former NBA player.

    Devin Booker grew up idolizing his father, who inspired him to pursue a basketball career.

    Booker’s father is African American, whereas his mother is Mexican/Puerto Rican.

    His parents never married but co-parented the NBA player throughout childhood and adolescence. It is worth saying that Booker’s parents have been by his side throughout.

    Melvin has played a pivotal role in shaping his son into a top player. He also played in the NBA for a short stint, spanning just 32 games.

    Melvin made his way overseas and played for teams in Italy, as well as Turkey, and Russia. In 2008, he announced his retirement from professional basketball. 

    Devin has two half-siblings, Davon Wade and Mya Powell. The three of them were born from the same mother but different fathers.

    However, they get along well and seem to support each other in every situation.

    Davon is the oldest, the NBA star is the middle child, and Mya is the youngest.

    Mya Powell suffers from a medical condition named Microdeletion syndrome. That means she has neurodevelopmental disabilities.

    Similarly, Davon works as a real estate professional at LinehanWADE Group.
