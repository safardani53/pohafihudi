    KATIE Price’s boobs have been branded “ridiculous” by her own mum Amy.

    The model has faced criticism for her numerous boob jobs but Amy, 70, says it is a cry for help.

    Joining Holly Willoughby and Craig Doyle on the This Morning sofa today, Amy discussed Katie’s surgery.

    She said: “Her boobs are ridiculous if you ask me,” while opening up on her new brand new book The Last Word, which has been serialised in The Sun.

    Amy continued to tell the TV hosts that her 45-year-old daughter now hides her operations from her in a bid to stop her from cancelling them.

    She explained: “I don’t want her to have any surgery at all and I cancelled a few operations so she couldn’t get her boobs done, but then she learnt not to tell me.

    “I can almost tell you now when she’s going to have something done. It’s body dysmorphia, and the tattoos are all a part of it too.”

    Amy said she remembers the moment when it all changed, when her daughter was having a photoshoot on the beach.

    Someone questioned Katie’s ability to be a Page 3 girl at the time, saying her boobs “weren’t big enough”.

    Amy told Holly and Craig that Katie had overheard the comments, adding: “All the family had a normal bust size but she was adamant then she needed bigger boobs and that’s how it all started.”

    Amy has discussed mum-of-five Katie’s surgery at length in the book, saying: “The natural 32B boobs my daughter had as a 17-year-old have got bigger and bigger until I barely recognise the body in front of me.

    “Some of the things she has done to her body are not only ridiculous, but dangerous.”

    Katie went under the knife back in December in a bid to get the "biggest breasts in Britain".

    The TV personality underwent her 16th boob job to make her assets larger than ever. 

    Before her operation, a source told The Sun: "She wants to have the biggest boobs in the country, and these will certainly set her on her way to that goal."

    The star is no stranger to cosmetic surgery, dating back to when she first went under the knife in 1998 to boost her boobs to a 32C.

    She had another breast enlargement in 1999, when she was known as Jordan, going up to a 32D and then a year later a 32G.

    After meeting her first husband, Peter Andre, she shrunk to a 32F in 2007 and then a 32D in 2008 - before going bigger again in the years to come.
