    COLLEGE basketball fans can fill their stomachs during March Madness with the help of six fast-food chains.

    The collage tournament is single-elimination and typically lasts for three weeks or longer.

    Tip-off begins this week and so do deals from your favorite fast-food chains.

    Plus, most of the cheap eats will last as long as the tournament does, giving fans time to order from multiple places.

    Foodies can try deals from other chains too as 7-Eleven is offering a large pizza and five-piece wings order for $10.

    For those with a sweat tooth, head to Baskin Robbins for its new flavor of the month - Chick'n & Waffles.

    Read More on Deals

    Below, we’ve rounded up six deals to enjoy while watching the big game.

    1. BURGER KING

    The burger joining has decided to bring back its Melts sandwiches- this time in three types: classic, spicy, and bacon.

    Though prices may change, each melt is typically around $7 including a drink and side. 

    To help celebrate, Burger King is offering Royal Perks members a change to enter its March Madness-themed "Royal Meltness" sweepstakes.

    Most read in Money

    Members will be fighting to win T-shirts, gaming consoles, and a $50,000 trip to Las Vegas. 

    2. HOOTERS 

    The wing giant is also offering bundles now through April 3.

    Fans can get 20 bone-in wings, 24 buffalo shrimp, and curly fries for $49.99.

    Or, customers can choose from 15 bone-in wings, 16 boneless wings, and curly fries for $39.99. 

    Those looking for a place to watch and win can head to Hooters as it’s hosting the 40th annual bracket challenge now through March 16.

    Customers may win trips to Dublin, Cancun, Las Vegas, and Florida.

    For those who don’t win, there’s still hope.

    If you place in the top bracket you'll each get a $40 gift card, and even those who entered and picked 60 or more NCAA tournament games correctly can win $40,000.

    3. PIZZA HUT

    Pizza Hut just brought back its 90s classic: mini basketballs with a pizza box that turns into a hoop.

    "We're back again for the seventh year as the official pizza of March Madness and we wanted to do something special for pizza-loving basketball fans," said Lindsay Morgan, CMO at Pizza Hut.

    The basketballs are now available nationwide via the Pizza Hut website and app for $7.

    The new design for the balls consists of a black and red pattern with the slogan "No One Out Pizzas The Hut."

    4. AUNTIE ANNE’S

    The pretzel giant is offering free delivery on all March Madness tournament game days - but you must order online or via the app.

    Plus, rewards members may participate in the Pretzel Mania challenge where if customers visit twice before April 5, they get a free original or cinnamon sugar pretzel.

    However, head into the store three times and you'll be entered for a prize pack including a pretzel bucket and Auntie Anne's swag.

    5. BUFFALO WILD WINGS

    Since wings and basketball are synonymous with each other, during the NCAA tournaments, Buffalo Wild Wings is adding some new flavors and great offers.

    Customers can choose from two new flavors: buffalo ranch and honey garlic, which is of blend two of its 26 signature sauces.

    Plus, if men's or women's games go into overtime, Blazin' Rewards members may get a free order of wings.

    This can be done by checking in at Buffalo Wild Wings on game day or placing an order online. 

    Six free boneless wings will then be delivered to your account the following day.

    6. UBER EATS

    Those looking to stay in and watch the game can still score some sweet deals with the delivery giant.

    Uber Eats has partnered with major chains with each round of the NCAA tournament.  

    Fans can grab the following:

    • March 14 and 15: Uber One members can get $5 off Papa John's when they spend $25 or more
    • March 18 to 20: Get a free grilled cheese burrito at Taco Bell with a $20 purchase
    • March 23 to 25: Get a free Chick-fil-A eight-count nugget order with a $15 purchase
    • March 26 and 27: Get 10 free wings from Wingstop with a $25 purchase
    • March 31 and April 1: Get a free Popeye's chicken sandwich when you spend $25 or more
    • April 2 and 3: Buy one Wendy's Dave's Single burger and get another for free
