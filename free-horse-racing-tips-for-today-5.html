    IGOR (12.45 Ascot) made a big impression when second on his debut here over 2m3f last month.

    He travelled strongly there and jumped well enough before looking very green when the race developed in earnest.

    He should appreciate this step up in distance and wouldn’t have to improve much to get off the mark.

    THOMAS DARBY (1.55 Ascot) won on his bumper debut and followed up when scoring at Cheltenham the first time he jumped a hurdle.

    He made one mistake that stopped him a little here last time but he was still beaten a neck in second.

    He is clearly still learning this game and should come on a lot, certainly enough to beat these rivals.

    MATTERHORN (2.05 Southwell) had plenty in hand when scoring at Lingfield last time out. He had two lengths in hand crossing the line but it could have been a lot more. He has a 6lb penalty today which should hold no fears over an ideal trip.

    VINNDICATION (2.30 Ascot) has done nothing but win so far, including on his chasing debut at Carlisle last time out.

    He jumped well enough there and kept pulling out more when challenged. He takes a slight hike in distance here which looks a wise move, and he has what it takes to go a long way as a chaser.
